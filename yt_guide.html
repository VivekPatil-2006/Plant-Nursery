🌿 User Guide

Welcome to our Plant Nursery! Here's how to explore and shop:

🌺 Indoor Plants

  • Click Indoor Plants → Choose Flowering or Non-Flowering
  • Browse plant list with images and names
  • Click View Details to see description and Buy Now to purchase

🌳 Outdoor Plants

  • Click Outdoor Plants → Choose Flowering or Non-Flowering
  • Browse plant list with images and names
  • Click View Details to see description and Buy Now to purchase

🛍️ Accessories

  • Click Accessories
  • Browse tools, pots, fertilizers, etc.
  • Click Buy Now to purchase

🧾 Payment

  • All purchases redirect to a secure payment gateway
  • Need help? Visit the Contact Us page.
  • Happy Gardening! 🌱
